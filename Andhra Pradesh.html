Welcome to Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh, the land of cultural diversity and natural beauty, offers everything from lush beaches to ancient temples and monuments. Explore cities like Visakhapatnam, Tirupati, and Vijayawada to experience the soul of South India.

Transportation to Andhra Pradesh

Travel options to Andhra Pradesh from LPU:

  • Visakhapatnam: ~1,700 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,000 (to Visakhapatnam Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (to Visakhapatnam Junction)
  • Tirupati: ~1,600 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,500 (to Tirupati Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,400 - ₹2,500 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹1,800 (to Tirupati Railway Station)
  • Vijayawada: ~1,650 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹6,500 (to Vijayawada Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,400 - ₹2,500 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,100 - ₹1,800 (to Vijayawada Junction)
  • Amaravati: ~1,700 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,000 (to Gannavaram Airport, Vijayawada)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (to Vijayawada Junction, 30 minutes drive to Amaravati)

Top Destinations in Andhra Pradesh

Visakhapatnam

Known as the "City of Destiny," Visakhapatnam boasts pristine beaches, hills, and bustling markets. Visit the famous RK Beach, Kailasagiri Hill, and the Submarine Museum.

Tirupati

Tirupati is home to the renowned Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, attracting millions of devotees annually. The town is also surrounded by hills, waterfalls, and rich cultural heritage.

Vijayawada

Famous for its temples, including the Kanaka Durga Temple, and the majestic Prakasam Barrage, Vijayawada is a hub for culture and tourism in the state.

Amaravati

Amaravati, the capital city of Andhra Pradesh, is known for the ancient Buddhist Amaravati Stupa and the new modern architecture being developed around it.
