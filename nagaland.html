Welcome to Nagaland

Nagaland, known for its vibrant cultures and traditions, is a northeastern state in India that offers breathtaking landscapes and unique tribal heritage. Discover the enchanting beauty of Nagaland!

Transportation to Nagaland

Travel options to reach Nagaland from LPU:

  • Kohima: ~2,350 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹7,000 (to Dimapur Airport) + cab to Kohima
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 (approximately 30-36 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Dimapur Railway Station) + cab to Kohima
  • Dimapur: ~2,370 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,500 (to Dimapur Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 30-36 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Dimapur Railway Station)
  • Mon: ~2,400 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹7,500 (to Dimapur Airport) + cab to Mon
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 30-36 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,600 (to Dimapur Railway Station) + cab to Mon
  • Wokha: ~2,380 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹7,000 (to Dimapur Airport) + cab to Wokha
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 (approximately 30-36 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Dimapur Railway Station) + cab to Wokha

Choose your preferred mode of transportation to explore the diverse cultures and landscapes of Nagaland!

Top Destinations in Nagaland

Kohima

The state capital known for its historical significance and vibrant culture.

Dimapur

The largest city in Nagaland, known for its rich heritage and economic hub.

Mon

Famous for its tribal culture and the traditional festivals of the Konyak tribe.

Wokha

Known for its beautiful landscapes and as the home of the Lotha tribe.
