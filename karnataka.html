Welcome to Karnataka

Karnataka, a southern state in India, is renowned for its vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, rich history, and iconic architectural landmarks. From the royal palaces of Mysore to the serene beaches of Gokarna, Karnataka offers something for every traveler!

Transportation to Karnataka

Travel options to Karnataka from LPU:

  • Bengaluru (Bangalore): ~1,800 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (to Kempegowda International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (approximately 30-35 hours)
    • Train: ₹700 - ₹1,500 (to Bengaluru City Railway Station)
  • Mysuru (Mysore): ~1,780 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Mysore Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 28-33 hours)
    • Train: ₹700 - ₹1,300 (to Mysore Railway Station)
  • Hampi: ~1,850 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹5,500 (to Hubli Airport, then 150 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹700 - ₹1,500 (to Hospet Railway Station, then 10 km by road)
  • Gokarna: ~1,900 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹5,500 (to Dabolim Airport, Goa, then 60 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (approximately 40-45 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Gokarna Road Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Karnataka

Bengaluru (Bangalore)

The Silicon Valley of India, known for its tech industry, beautiful parks, and vibrant nightlife. Visit the Lalbagh Botanical Garden and the iconic Bengaluru Palace.

Mysuru (Mysore)

A city steeped in royal history, home to the majestic Mysore Palace and the vibrant Dasara festival. Don't miss the Chamundeshwari Temple and the nearby Brindavan Gardens.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Hampi is famous for its ancient ruins, temples, and bouldered landscapes. Explore the Virupaksha Temple and the ruins of Vijayanagara Empire.

Gokarna

A serene coastal town known for its pristine beaches and temples. Gokarna is an offbeat destination that attracts pilgrims and backpackers alike.
