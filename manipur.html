Welcome to Manipur

Manipur is a state in Northeast India, renowned for its natural beauty, lush green hills, and unique culture. It's famous for the Loktak Lake, its rich dance traditions, and beautiful landscapes.

Transportation to Manipur

Travel options to Manipur from LPU:

  • Imphal: ~2,400 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹7,000 - ₹10,000 (to Imphal Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 (to nearby railhead and then bus to Imphal)

Top Destinations in Manipur

Loktak Lake

Loktak Lake is the largest freshwater lake in northeastern India, famous for its unique floating islands and stunning natural beauty. It is a popular tourist destination for its peaceful atmosphere and biodiversity.

Imphal

Imphal, the capital city of Manipur, is known for its historical significance, beautiful temples, and lush green surroundings. It's also a center for cultural heritage and traditional festivals.

Sangai National Park

Sangai National Park is famous for the endangered Manipur brow-antlered deer, known as Sangai. The park offers a unique opportunity for wildlife enthusiasts to see rare species in their natural habitat.

Kangla Fort

The Kangla Fort is an ancient historical site in Imphal that was once the seat of the Manipur Kingdom. It holds significant cultural and architectural value and is a must-visit for history lovers.
