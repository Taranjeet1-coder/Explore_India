Welcome to Meghalaya

Meghalaya, known as the "Abode of Clouds," is a picturesque state in northeastern India, famous for its breathtaking hills, lush green landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Experience the beauty and tranquility of Meghalaya!

Transportation to Meghalaya

Travel options to reach Meghalaya from LPU:

  • Shillong: ~2,000 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,500 (to Guwahati Airport) + ₹2,000 (cab to Shillong)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 (approximately 30-36 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Guwahati Railway Station) + ₹2,000 (cab to Shillong)
  • Cherrapunji: ~2,050 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,500 (to Guwahati Airport) + ₹2,500 (cab to Cherrapunji)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 32-38 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Guwahati Railway Station) + ₹2,500 (cab to Cherrapunji)

Choose your preferred mode of transportation to explore the stunning beauty and unique culture of Meghalaya!

Top Destinations in Meghalaya

Shillong

The capital city known for its pleasant climate and scenic views.

Cherrapunji

Famous for its stunning waterfalls and living root bridges.

Mawsmai Cave

A popular tourist spot known for its limestone caves and adventure.

Entry Fee: ₹100 per person (for Indian tourists)

Laitlum Canyons

Offers breathtaking views and hiking opportunities.

Entry Fee: ₹20 per person.
