Welcome to Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is known for its rich historical, cultural, and religious heritage. Home to iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal, Varanasi ghats, and Lucknow’s Mughal architecture, it’s a must-visit state for every traveler.

Transportation to Uttar Pradesh

Travel options to Uttar Pradesh from LPU:

  • Agra: ~500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Agra Airport)
    • Bus: ₹800 - ₹1,300 (approximately 8-9 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Agra Railway Station)
  • Varanasi: ~700 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (to Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,600 (approximately 12-14 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Varanasi Railway Station)
  • Lucknow: ~450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,200 (approximately 7-8 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Lucknow Railway Station)
  • Kanpur: ~450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Kanpur Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,100 (approximately 8 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Kanpur Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Uttar Pradesh

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of eternal love. Its stunning white marble architecture attracts millions of visitors every year.

Varanasi Ghats

Known as the spiritual capital of India, Varanasi's ghats are famous for their daily rituals, offering a unique experience of Hindu spirituality and culture.

Lucknow

Famous for its Mughal architecture, Lucknow is home to stunning landmarks such as the Bara Imambara, Rumi Darwaza, and the Chota Imambara.

Kanpur

Kanpur is known for its industrial significance and historic landmarks, such as the Allen Forest Zoo and the Kanpur Memorial Church.
