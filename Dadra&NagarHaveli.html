Welcome to Dadra & Nagar Haveli

Dadra & Nagar Haveli is a small Union Territory located between Gujarat and Maharashtra. Known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and tribal culture, it is an ideal destination for nature lovers and those looking to experience rural life.

Transportation to Dadra & Nagar Haveli

Travel options to Dadra & Nagar Haveli from LPU:

  • Silvassa (Capital of Dadra & Nagar Haveli): ~1,600 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹7,000 (to Daman Airport, then by road to Silvassa)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹2,500 (to Vapi Railway Station, then by road to Silvassa)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 (approximately 30-35 hours)

Top Destinations in Dadra & Nagar Haveli

Silvassa

Silvassa is the capital of Dadra & Nagar Haveli and is known for its peaceful environment, tribal culture, and natural beauty. Visit the vibrant tribal villages, peaceful lakes, and the beautiful Dudhni Lake.

Khadoli Wildlife Sanctuary

This sanctuary is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts, home to diverse flora and fauna including the leopard, hyena, and various species of birds. It is a great place for trekking and nature walks.

Maidan Giri

Situated atop a hill, Maidan Giri offers a panoramic view of the surrounding area. It is famous for its serene atmosphere and is ideal for nature walks and photography.

Vanganga Lake

Vanganga Lake is a beautiful spot surrounded by lush greenery and is a peaceful place for boating and picnics. The lake is located near the Vanganga Garden, which is also a popular tourist spot.
