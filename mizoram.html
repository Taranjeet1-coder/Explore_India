Welcome to Mizoram

Mizoram, nestled in the hills of northeastern India, is known for its serene landscapes, vibrant festivals, and welcoming people. Explore the charm and beauty of Mizoram!

Transportation to Mizoram

Options to travel to Mizoram's major destinations from LPU:

  • Aizawl: ~2,800 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹6,500 - ₹11,000
    • Train + Bus: ₹2,200 - ₹4,000 (closest railway station is Silchar, followed by a road journey)
  • Lunglei: ~2,850 km from LPU
    • Flight to Aizawl + Drive: ₹7,000 - ₹12,000
    • Train to Silchar + Road Journey: ₹2,500 - ₹5,000
  • Champhai: ~2,900 km from LPU
    • Flight to Aizawl + Drive: ₹8,000 - ₹13,000
    • Train to Silchar + Road Journey: ₹3,000 - ₹6,000

Select your preferred route to experience Mizoram's scenic beauty and vibrant culture!
