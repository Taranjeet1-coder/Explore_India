Welcome to Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is a state known for its natural beauty, tribal culture, and historical significance. Explore dense forests, waterfalls, and ancient temples that make Chhattisgarh an ideal destination for nature lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Transportation to Chhattisgarh

Travel options to Chhattisgarh from LPU:

  • Raipur: ~1,200 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹6,000 (to Raipur Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,500 (approximately 24-28 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,500 (to Raipur Junction)
  • Bilaspur: ~1,220 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,000 (to Raipur, then 125 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹750 - ₹1,700 (approximately 25-30 hours)
  • Durg: ~1,230 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,500 (to Raipur, then 40 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹750 - ₹1,700 (approximately 26-31 hours)
  • Raigarh: ~1,240 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,000 - ₹8,000 (to Raipur, then 160 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹800 - ₹1,800 (approximately 27-32 hours)

Top Destinations in Chhattisgarh

Raipur

Raipur, the capital city of Chhattisgarh, is a growing urban center with a rich cultural heritage. It is also a gateway to several nearby tourist destinations like Kanger Valley and Chitrakote Waterfalls.

Bilaspur

Bilaspur is known for its vibrant culture and scenic views. The city is home to several temples and nearby natural wonders such as the Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary.

Durg

Durg is a historical town famous for its forts and temples, including the famous Durg Fort. It is surrounded by beautiful hills and forests.

Raigarh

Raigarh is famous for its majestic Raigarh Fort, and it is also known for its beautiful tribal culture and art forms, making it a cultural hub in the state.
