Welcome to Haryana

Haryana, a northern state in India, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and agricultural prominence. Explore the dynamic cities and serene villages of Haryana!

Transportation to Haryana

Travel options to reach Haryana from LPU:

  • Chandigarh: ~350 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹2,500 - ₹5,000 (to Chandigarh Airport)
    • Bus: ₹500 - ₹1,200 (approximately 6-8 hours)
    • Train: ₹300 - ₹800 (nearest station is Ambala)
  • Gurgaon: ~400 km from LPU
    • Flight to Delhi + Cab: ₹2,500 - ₹4,500
    • Bus: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (approximately 8-10 hours)
    • Train: ₹400 - ₹900 (to Gurgaon Railway Station)
  • Faridabad: ~420 km from LPU
    • Flight to Delhi + Cab: ₹2,800 - ₹4,800
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,500 (approximately 8-10 hours)
    • Train: ₹300 - ₹700 (to Faridabad Railway Station)

Choose your preferred mode of transportation to experience the culture and hospitality of Haryana!
