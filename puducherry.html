Welcome to Puducherry

Puducherry, a charming coastal town in southern India, is a blend of French colonial architecture, pristine beaches, and a rich cultural heritage. The relaxed vibe and scenic landscapes make it an ideal getaway for travelers looking for peace and exploration.

Transportation to Puducherry

Travel options to Puducherry from LPU:

  • Puducherry: ~2,100 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,000 - ₹8,000 (to Chennai International Airport)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,500 (to Villupuram Railway Station, approx. 35 km from Puducherry)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,200 (approximately 35-40 hours by bus)

From Chennai, Puducherry is approximately 150 km away, and you can travel by taxi, bus, or private car in around 3 hours.

Top Destinations in Puducherry

Serenity Beach

Serenity Beach is a peaceful, less-crowded beach that offers scenic views and a perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing.

Auroville

Auroville is a unique international community dedicated to human unity. The Matrimandir, a golden globe-shaped structure, is the centerpiece of Auroville and offers a tranquil atmosphere for meditation.

Promenade Beach

Promenade Beach is a famous beach in Puducherry with a long stretch of coastline lined with palm trees. It’s a great place to enjoy a peaceful walk along the shore.
