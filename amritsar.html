  • Train: ₹500 - ₹1000 (depends on class and distance)

Plan your trip accordingly to experience the vibrant culture of Amritsar!

Top Destinations in Amritsar

Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

The spiritual heart of the Sikh community, known for its stunning architecture and serene atmosphere.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historical park memorializing the tragic massacre of 1919, with a haunting beauty and deep significance.

Wagah Border

Famous for its daily flag-lowering ceremony, a display of patriotism from both Indian and Pakistani sides.

Summer Palace (Ram Bagh)

A beautiful garden and historic palace where Maharaja Ranjit Singh spent his summers, featuring stunning landscaping.

Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum

Showcasing the rich heritage of the Sikh Empire with artifacts, paintings, and personal items of the Maharaja.
