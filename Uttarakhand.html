Welcome to Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, known as the "Land of Gods," is a breathtakingly beautiful state in northern India, famous for its scenic landscapes, tranquil temples, and spiritual heritage. The state offers a perfect blend of adventure and spirituality, with the Himalayas as its backdrop.

Transportation to Uttarakhand

Travel options to Uttarakhand from LPU:

  • Nainital: ~350 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Pantnagar Airport, then a short drive)
    • Bus: ₹800 - ₹1,400 (approximately 8-9 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Kathgodam Railway Station, then a drive)
  • Rishikesh: ~230 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹2,500 - ₹4,500 (to Jolly Grant Airport)
    • Bus: ₹500 - ₹900 (approximately 6-7 hours)
    • Train: ₹400 - ₹800 (to Haridwar Railway Station, then a drive)
  • Mussoorie: ~300 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Jolly Grant Airport, then a drive)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,200 (approximately 7-8 hours)
    • Train: ₹400 - ₹900 (to Dehradun Railway Station, then a drive)

Top Destinations in Uttarakhand

Nainital

Nainital is a picturesque hill station known for its beautiful Naini Lake, boat rides, and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. It’s a perfect spot for relaxation and nature walks.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh, also known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a major spiritual center, famous for its temples, the Ganga Aarti, and adventure activities like white-water rafting and trekking.

Mussoorie

Mussoorie, known as the "Queen of the Hills," offers scenic views of the Doon Valley and the Himalayan ranges. Popular attractions include Kempty Falls, Gun Hill, and Camel's Back Road.
