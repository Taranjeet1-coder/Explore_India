Welcome to Assam

Assam, located in the northeastern part of India, is known for its lush tea gardens, the mighty Brahmaputra River, and a rich cultural heritage. From the UNESCO-listed Kaziranga National Park to the vibrant festivals like Bihu, Assam is a true paradise for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts.

Transportation to Assam

Travel options to Assam from LPU:

  • Guwahati: ~1,200 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹7,000 (to Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 20-24 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Guwahati Railway Station)
  • Kaziranga: ~1,300 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹6,500 (to Guwahati, then 4-hour drive to Kaziranga)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,000 (approximately 24-26 hours)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹1,500 (to Jorhat or Kaziranga Railway Station, then taxi to the park)
  • Tezpur: ~1,100 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (to Guwahati, then 3-hour drive to Tezpur)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 22-24 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,300 (to Tezpur Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Assam

Guwahati

Guwahati, the gateway to Assam, is a vibrant city known for its temples, Brahmaputra River views, and rich cultural heritage. Key attractions include Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Island, and the Assam State Museum.

Kaziranga National Park

Known for its population of one-horned rhinoceroses, Kaziranga is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It offers a thrilling safari experience with diverse wildlife, including tigers, elephants, and deer.

Tezpur

Tezpur, often called the 'Cultural Capital of Assam,' is known for its historical temples, ancient ruins, and picturesque views of the Brahmaputra River. Major attractions include the Agnigarh Fort and the picturesque Nameri National Park.
