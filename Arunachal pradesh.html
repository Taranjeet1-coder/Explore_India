Welcome to Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh, located in the northeastern part of India, is known for its picturesque landscapes, beautiful monasteries, and diverse tribal culture. It offers lush green hills, valleys, and rivers, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

Transportation to Arunachal Pradesh

Travel options to Arunachal Pradesh from LPU:

  • Itanagar: ~2,100 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,000 - ₹8,000 (to Lilabari Airport, Assam)
    • Bus: ₹1,800 - ₹3,000 (approximately 50-55 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (to Naharlagun Railway Station, around 20 km from Itanagar)
  • Tawang: ~2,300 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹6,000 - ₹9,000 (to Tezpur Airport, Assam)
    • Bus: ₹2,000 - ₹3,500 (approximately 55-60 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,500 - ₹2,800 (to Tezpur Railway Station, around 185 km from Tawang)
  • Bomdila: ~2,200 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,500 - ₹8,000 (to Tezpur Airport, Assam)
    • Bus: ₹1,800 - ₹3,000 (approximately 50-55 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,500 - ₹2,800 (to Tezpur Railway Station, around 120 km from Bomdila)

Top Destinations in Arunachal Pradesh

Itanagar

Itanagar, the capital city of Arunachal Pradesh, is surrounded by lush green hills and offers beautiful views of the Himalayas. The city is home to historical sites, including the Ita Fort and the Ganga Lake.

Tawang

Tawang, known for the Tawang Monastery, is a serene hill station offering stunning views of snow-capped mountains and rich Buddhist culture. It is a must-visit destination for nature and spirituality enthusiasts.

Bomdila

Bomdila is a beautiful town surrounded by pine forests and apple orchards, offering breathtaking views of the Eastern Himalayas. It is a perfect destination for trekking and nature walks.
