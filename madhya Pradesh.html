Welcome to Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, known as the heart of India, is a state rich in culture, history, and natural beauty. From the ancient temples of Khajuraho to the lush forests of Kanha National Park, there’s much to explore and discover.

Transportation to Madhya Pradesh

Travel options to Madhya Pradesh from LPU:

  • Bhopal: ~1,450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹2,500 - ₹4,000 (to Raja Bhoj Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 (approximately 24-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Bhopal Railway Station)
  • Indore: ~1,420 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹2,500 - ₹4,000 (to Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 24-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Indore Railway Station)
  • Khajuraho: ~1,500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Khajuraho Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,000 (approximately 30-35 hours)
    • Train: ₹700 - ₹1,300 (to Khajuraho Railway Station)
  • Kanha National Park: ~1,600 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Jabalpur Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 35-40 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Jabalpur Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Madhya Pradesh

Bhopal

The capital city, known for its serene lakes, rich history, and vibrant culture. Don't miss the Taj-ul-Masajid and the historical Bhojpur Temple.

Indore

Known for its food, vibrant markets, and proximity to historical attractions like Mandu and Maheshwar.

Khajuraho

A UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its stunning temples with intricate erotic carvings and architectural splendor.

Kanha National Park

One of India's most famous wildlife sanctuaries, home to tigers, leopards, and the rare Barasingha deer.
