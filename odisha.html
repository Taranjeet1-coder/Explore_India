Welcome to Odisha

Odisha, known for its ancient temples, scenic beaches, and vibrant culture, is a captivating state in eastern India. Discover the beauty and heritage of Odisha!

Transportation to Odisha

Options to travel to Odisha's major destinations from LPU:

  • Bhubaneswar: ~1,700 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,500 - ₹9,500
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹2,200
    • Bus (multiple stops): ₹3,500 - ₹5,000
  • Puri: ~1,750 km from LPU
    • Flight to Bhubaneswar + Drive: ₹6,000 - ₹10,000
    • Train: ₹1,200 - ₹2,500
  • Konark: ~1,730 km from LPU
    • Flight to Bhubaneswar + Drive: ₹6,000 - ₹10,000
    • Train: ₹1,200 - ₹2,500
