Welcome to Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh, a breathtaking hill state in northern India, is known for its snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, trekking routes, and beautiful hill stations. From Shimla to Manali, Dharamshala to Kullu, it offers a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Transportation to Himachal Pradesh

Travel options to Himachal Pradesh from LPU:

  • Shimla: ~350 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Shimla Airport)
    • Bus: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (approximately 7-8 hours)
    • Train: ₹400 - ₹800 (to Kalka Railway Station, then 5-hour bus ride to Shimla)
  • Manali: ~500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,500 (to Kullu-Manali Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,400 (approximately 12-14 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Pathankot Railway Station, then 5-hour bus ride to Manali)
  • Dharamshala: ~450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Gaggal Airport, Dharamshala)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,300 (approximately 10-12 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Pathankot Railway Station, then 2-hour bus ride)
  • Kullu: ~500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹6,000 (to Kullu-Manali Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,200 (approximately 12-14 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Pathankot Railway Station, then 5-hour bus ride)

Top Destinations in Himachal Pradesh

Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is known for its colonial architecture, pleasant weather, and panoramic views. Popular attractions include the Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and Kufri.

Manali

Manali is a popular hill station known for its snow-capped mountains, adventure sports, and scenic beauty. Activities like trekking, paragliding, and river rafting make it a must-visit for adventure seekers.

Dharamshala

Dharamshala is famous for being the home of the Dalai Lama and its Tibetan culture. The lush green surroundings, trekking routes, and peaceful ambiance make it a perfect destination for peace and spirituality.

Kullu

Kullu is known for its temples, valleys, and adventure activities. The Kullu Valley is famous for its apple orchards, and the Kullu Dussehra festival is a major attraction.
