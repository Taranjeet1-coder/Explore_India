Welcome to Goa

Goa, known for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, is a popular travel destination for people from all over the world. Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or culture, Goa offers a diverse range of experiences for every traveler.

Transportation to Goa

Travel options to Goa from LPU:

  • North Goa (e.g., Baga Beach): ~1,800 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,000 (to Goa International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,200 (approximately 28-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹1,500 (to Madgaon Railway Station or Thivim Railway Station)
  • South Goa (e.g., Palolem Beach): ~1,850 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹6,500 (to Goa International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,800 - ₹2,400 (approximately 30-32 hours)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹1,500 (to Madgaon Railway Station, followed by a short drive)

Top Destinations in Goa

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its vibrant nightlife, water sports, and beach shacks. It is the perfect destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Old Goa

Old Goa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is known for its colonial architecture, including historic churches and cathedrals like the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral.

Dudhsagar Falls

Dudhsagar Falls, one of the tallest waterfalls in India, is located in the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary. The majestic waterfall is a popular spot for trekkers and nature lovers.

Palolem Beach

Located in South Goa, Palolem Beach is known for its beautiful crescent-shaped coastline, calm waters, and laid-back vibe, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and yoga retreats.
