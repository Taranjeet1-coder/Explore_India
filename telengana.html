Welcome to Telangana

Telangana, a state in southern India, is known for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and IT hub in Hyderabad. Explore the many facets of Telangana, from ancient forts to modern marvels.

Top Destinations in Telangana

Hyderabad

The capital city, known for its historical sites, IT industry, and the famous Charminar.

Warangal

A historic city known for the Warangal Fort, temples, and the Kakatiya architecture.

Khammam

A scenic town with ancient forts and serene landscapes.

Nagarjuna Sagar

Famous for the Nagarjuna Sagar Dam and beautiful lake views.

Ramagundam

Known for its industrial significance and lush surroundings.
