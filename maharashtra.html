Welcome to Maharashtra

Maharashtra is a vibrant state in western India, famous for its rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and historical landmarks. From bustling Mumbai to the serene beaches of Konkan, explore the diverse beauty of Maharashtra!

Transportation to Maharashtra

Travel options to Maharashtra from LPU:

  • Mumbai: ~1,500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,800 (approximately 24-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Mumbai CST)
  • Pune: ~1,490 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Pune Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 26-32 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Pune Railway Station)
  • Aurangabad: ~1,450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Aurangabad Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 26-32 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Aurangabad Railway Station)
  • Nashik: ~1,480 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Nashik Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800 (approximately 25-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹600 - ₹1,200 (to Nashik Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Maharashtra

Mumbai

The financial capital of India, known for its iconic Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and vibrant nightlife.

Pune

A cultural hub with historic forts, beautiful gardens, and a thriving culinary scene.

Aurangabad

Famous for the Ajanta and Ellora caves, UNESCO World Heritage Sites showcasing ancient rock-cut architecture.

Nashik

Known for its vineyards, temples, and the Kumbh Mela festival, offering a unique blend of spirituality and tourism.
