Welcome to Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture.

Transportation to Jaipur

If you're traveling from Home to Jaipur, here are the estimated costs:

  • Bus: ₹500 - ₹800 (depending on the bus service)
  • Train: ₹300 - ₹1500 (depends on class and distance)
  • Flight: ₹2000 - ₹5000 (depending on the distance and booking time)

Top Destinations in Jaipur

Amer Fort

A stunning fort located on a hilltop, known for its intricate architecture and beautiful views.

Hawa Mahal

Also known as the Palace of Winds, this iconic structure features a unique façade with numerous windows.

City Palace

The royal residence that showcases a blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture.

Jal Mahal

A beautiful palace situated in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, offering picturesque views.

Nahargarh Fort

Perched on the Aravalli hills, this fort provides stunning panoramic views of the Pink City.
