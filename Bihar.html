Welcome to Bihar

Bihar, the land of historical significance, is home to landmarks like Bodh Gaya, Patna, Nalanda, and Rajgir. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Bihar is a blend of ancient history and modern vibrancy.

Transportation to Bihar

Travel options to Bihar from LPU:

  • Patna: ~1,400 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,100 - ₹2,000 (approximately 28-32 hours)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹1,600 (to Patna Junction)
  • Bodh Gaya: ~1,450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (to Gaya Airport, then 20 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,300 (approximately 30-35 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹1,800 (to Gaya Junction, then 10 km by road)
  • Nalanda: ~1,420 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Patna, then 20 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,200 (approximately 30-35 hours)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹1,600 (to Nalanda Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Bihar

Patna

The capital city of Bihar, Patna is rich in history and modern culture. Visit Patna Sahib, the birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh, and enjoy the Ganga Aarti at Gandhi Ghat.

Bodh Gaya

Bodh Gaya is one of the holiest Buddhist sites in the world. The Mahabodhi Temple, where Buddha attained enlightenment, is a major pilgrimage destination.

Nalanda

Known for the ancient Nalanda University, a center of learning in medieval India, Nalanda offers a glimpse into India's intellectual history.
