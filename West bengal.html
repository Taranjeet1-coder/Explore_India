Welcome to West Bengal

West Bengal, located in the eastern part of India, is a land of rich history, culture, and diversity. Known for its iconic cultural festivals, tea gardens, the majestic Sundarbans, and the colonial charm of Kolkata, West Bengal offers a vibrant mix of traditions, art, and natural beauty.

Transportation to West Bengal

Travel options to West Bengal from LPU:

  • Kolkata: ~1,500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,000 - ₹8,000 (to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 25-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹2,500 (to Howrah Railway Station)
  • Darjeeling: ~1,000 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹6,000 (to Bagdogra Airport, then a drive)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,800 (approximately 20-24 hours)

    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,000 (to Kolkata, then a drive or ferry)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 (approximately 22-26 hours)

    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹6,500 (to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport, then a drive)

Top Destinations in West Bengal

Kolkata

Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, is a city of vibrant heritage and traditions. It is famous for its colonial architecture, vibrant festivals, and iconic landmarks like the Howrah Bridge, Victoria Memorial, and Dakshineswar Kali Temple.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling, also known as the "Queen of the Hills," offers breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga, lush tea gardens, and a serene atmosphere. Don’t miss the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Sundarbans

The Sundarbans is a UNESCO World Heritage site and the largest mangrove forest in the world. It is home to the majestic Bengal tiger and an incredible array of flora and fauna. A boat ride through its waterways is a unique experience.

Shantiniketan

Shantiniketan is a tranquil town known for its connection to Rabindranath Tagore. The Visva Bharati University here is famous for its unique style of education and cultural heritage.
