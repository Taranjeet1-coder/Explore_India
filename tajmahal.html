Welcome to the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. This iconic symbol of love was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is known for its stunning white marble architecture and its serene gardens, making it one of the most visited and photographed monuments in the world.

Taj Mahal Details

About the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble mausoleum, built by Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Its symmetrical design, lush gardens, and intricate carvings make it a masterpiece of Mughal architecture. It is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful buildings in the world.

Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is cool and pleasant. Summer months (April to June) can get very hot, with temperatures rising above 40°C.

Interior of the Taj Mahal

The interior of the Taj Mahal features an intricate marble inlay work and precious gemstones. The central tomb houses the tombs of Shah Jahan and Mumtaz Mahal. The tomb is surrounded by a beautiful prayer hall, decorated with calligraphy and floral patterns.
