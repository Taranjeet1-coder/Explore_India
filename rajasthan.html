Welcome to Rajasthan

Rajasthan, known as the "Land of Kings," is famous for its rich history, majestic palaces, forts, desert landscapes, and vibrant culture. A journey through Rajasthan is like stepping back in time to experience India's royal heritage.

Transportation to Rajasthan

Travel options to Rajasthan from LPU:

  • Jaipur: ~500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹2,500 - ₹5,000 (to Jaipur International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,200 (approximately 8-10 hours)
    • Train: ₹400 - ₹1,000 (to Jaipur Railway Station)
  • Udaipur: ~700 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,500 (to Maharana Pratap Airport)
    • Bus: ₹900 - ₹1,500 (approximately 12-14 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,200 (to Udaipur Railway Station)
  • Jodhpur: ~600 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Jodhpur Airport)
    • Bus: ₹800 - ₹1,300 (approximately 10-12 hours)
    • Train: ₹500 - ₹1,000 (to Jodhpur Railway Station)
  • Jaisalmer: ~800 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹7,000 (to Jaisalmer Airport, connecting flights via Jaipur)

Top Destinations in Rajasthan

Amber Fort, Jaipur

Amber Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of the most iconic attractions in Rajasthan. Its stunning architecture, beautiful courtyards, and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes make it a must-visit destination.

Lake Palace, Udaipur

The Lake Palace, located on Lake Pichola, is a symbol of royal luxury and beauty. The palace is an architectural marvel and provides a serene and romantic atmosphere perfect for relaxation and sightseeing.

Mehrangarh Fort, Jodhpur

Mehrangarh is one of the largest and most magnificent forts in India. It offers stunning views of the Blue City of Jodhpur and houses an extensive collection of artifacts, paintings, and historical exhibits.

Jaisalmer Sand Dunes

Experience the beauty of the Thar Desert at the Jaisalmer Sand Dunes. Camel rides, traditional Rajasthani performances, and desert camping are popular activities for tourists visiting this golden city.
