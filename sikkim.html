Welcome to Sikkim

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, is a paradise known for its stunning mountain views, serene monasteries, and vibrant cultural heritage. Experience the beauty and tranquility of Sikkim.

Transportation to Sikkim

Options to travel to Sikkim's major destinations from LPU:

  • Gangtok: ~1,800 km from LPU
    • Flight to Bagdogra + Drive: ₹6,000 - ₹10,000
    • Train to Siliguri + Drive: ₹1,000 - ₹2,500
    • Bus (multiple stops): ₹3,500 - ₹5,000
  • Pelling: ~1,850 km from LPU
    • Flight to Bagdogra + Drive: ₹6,500 - ₹10,500
    • Train to Siliguri + Drive: ₹1,200 - ₹2,800
  • Lachung: ~1,900 km from LPU
    • Flight to Bagdogra + Drive: ₹7,000 - ₹11,000
    • Train to Siliguri + Drive: ₹1,300 - ₹3,000

Choose the travel option that best suits your adventure to explore Sikkim's serene beauty!

Top Destinations in Sikkim

Gangtok

The capital city known for its stunning views of Mount Kanchenjunga, monasteries, and vibrant marketplaces.

Pelling

A charming town offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas, monasteries, and waterfalls.

Lachung

Known for the Yumthang Valley and its beautiful alpine scenery, often called the "Valley of Flowers".
