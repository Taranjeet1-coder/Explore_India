Welcome to Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir, located in the northernmost region of India, is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, serene lakes, and snow-capped mountains. It is a paradise for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking peace amidst majestic beauty.

Transportation to Jammu & Kashmir

Travel options to Jammu & Kashmir from LPU:

  • Srinagar (Capital of Jammu & Kashmir): ~1,100 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹5,000 - ₹8,000 (to Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport, Srinagar)
    • Train: ₹1,200 - ₹2,500 (to Jammu Tawi Railway Station, then road to Srinagar)
    • Bus: ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 (approximately 20-22 hours)
  • Jammu (City): ~1,000 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,500 - ₹7,500 (to Jammu Airport)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹2,500 (to Jammu Railway Station)
    • Bus: ₹900 - ₹1,800 (approximately 18-20 hours)

Top Destinations in Jammu & Kashmir

Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir, is known for its beautiful Dal Lake, houseboats, and Mughal gardens. It’s a haven for tourists seeking a peaceful getaway.

Gulmarg

Famous for its ski resorts and lush green meadows, Gulmarg is the perfect destination for winter sports enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Leh-Ladakh

Leh, in the region of Ladakh, offers unparalleled scenic beauty with its arid mountains, pristine lakes, and Buddhist monasteries. It’s ideal for trekking and adventure tourism.

Vaishno Devi

A major pilgrimage destination, Vaishno Devi Temple attracts millions of devotees annually. It is located atop Trikuta Mountain and offers a spiritually enriching experience.
