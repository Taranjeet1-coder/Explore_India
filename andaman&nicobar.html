Welcome to Andaman & Nicobar Islands

The Andaman & Nicobar Islands are an exotic paradise located in the Bay of Bengal, known for their pristine beaches, coral reefs, and rich biodiversity. Whether you’re looking to relax on sun-kissed shores or explore vibrant marine life, this archipelago is the perfect destination for all kinds of travelers.

Transportation to Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Travel options to Andaman & Nicobar Islands from LPU:

  • Port Blair (Capital of Andaman & Nicobar Islands): ~2,500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹7,000 - ₹15,000 (to Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair)
    • Train and Ship: ₹1,500 - ₹3,000 (Train to Kolkata or Chennai, then ship to Port Blair)

From Port Blair, you can take ferries to various islands in the Andaman & Nicobar group.

Top Destinations in Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Havelock Island

Famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sand beaches, and vibrant coral reefs, Havelock Island is a popular spot for water activities like snorkeling and scuba diving.

Ross Island

A historically significant island with ruins from the British colonial era, Ross Island offers a peaceful atmosphere and stunning views of the surrounding waters.

Neil Island

Known for its secluded beaches and clear waters, Neil Island is perfect for a quiet retreat and some peaceful snorkeling or a boat ride.

Elephant Beach

Famous for its rich marine life and water sports activities, Elephant Beach is a must-visit for adventure enthusiasts looking for snorkeling, scuba diving, and glass-bottom boat rides.
