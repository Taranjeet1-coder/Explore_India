Top Destinations in Agra

Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this iconic mausoleum is a symbol of love and a must-visit in Agra.

Agra Fort

A historical fort known for its stunning architecture and rich history, offering beautiful views of the Taj Mahal.

Mehtab Bagh

A beautiful garden complex located across the Yamuna River, offering stunning views of the Taj Mahal at sunset.

Jama Masjid

A large mosque with beautiful architecture, known for its serene ambiance and stunning views of the city.

Fatehpur Sikri

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this city was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and showcases stunning architecture.
