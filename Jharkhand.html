Welcome to Jharkhand

Jharkhand, known for its lush forests, wildlife, and natural beauty, is a state in eastern India with vibrant tribal culture and breathtaking landscapes. From the scenic waterfalls of Ranchi to the spiritual energy of Baidyanath Temple, Jharkhand offers a unique experience for travelers!

Transportation to Jharkhand

Travel options to Jharkhand from LPU:

  • Ranchi: ~1,400 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (to Birsa Munda Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 25-30 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Ranchi Junction)
  • Jamshedpur: ~1,450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (nearest airport is Birsa Munda Airport, then 130 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 28-32 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Tatanagar Junction)
  • Deoghar: ~1,450 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,500 (nearest airport is Deoghar Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 (approximately 28-32 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Baidyanathdham Deoghar Station)

Choose your preferred mode of transportation and get ready to explore Jharkhand’s wonders!

Top Destinations in Jharkhand

Ranchi

Known as the "City of Waterfalls," Ranchi is a beautiful city filled with natural wonders like Dassam and Hundru Falls, alongside parks and scenic views from the Ranchi Hill.

Jamshedpur

The "Steel City of India," known for its well-planned infrastructure and green spaces like Jubilee Park and Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary, perfect for nature lovers.

Deoghar

A major pilgrimage site, Deoghar is home to the famous Baidyanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, and attracts millions of devotees every year.
