Welcome to Punjab

Punjab, the land of five rivers, is known for its rich culture, vibrant traditions, and historical landmarks. Whether it's the Golden Temple in Amritsar or the cultural beauty of Patiala, Punjab offers a unique experience to every traveler.

Transportation to Punjab

Travel options to Punjab from LPU:

  • Amritsar: ~250 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹2,500 - ₹4,500 (to Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹700 - ₹1,200 (approximately 5-6 hours)
    • Train: ₹400 - ₹900 (to Amritsar Railway Station)
  • Patiala: ~300 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Chandigarh Airport, then 60 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹600 - ₹1,000 (approximately 6-7 hours)
    • Train: ₹350 - ₹800 (to Patiala Railway Station)
  • Ludhiana: ~250 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (to Ludhiana Airport)
    • Bus: ₹600 - ₹1,000 (approximately 5-6 hours)
    • Train: ₹350 - ₹800 (to Ludhiana Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Punjab

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Golden Temple is one of the most sacred places for Sikhs. Its serene atmosphere and stunning architecture make it a must-visit destination in India.

Patiala

Known for its regal history and stunning palaces, Patiala is famous for the Qila Mubarak and its cultural heritage, including Patiala gharana music.

Ludhiana

Ludhiana is known as the Manchester of India due to its thriving textile industry and rich cultural diversity. Visit the Punjab Agricultural University Museum and the Maharaja Ranjit Singh War Memorial.
