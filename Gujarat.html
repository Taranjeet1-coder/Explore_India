Welcome to Gujarat

Gujarat, a vibrant state in western India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, historic sites, and thriving industries. From the architectural marvels of Ahmedabad to the serene white deserts of Kutch, Gujarat has something unique for every traveler!

Transportation to Gujarat

Travel options to Gujarat from LPU:

  • Ahmedabad: ~1,300 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,200 (24-28 hours)
    • Train: ₹900 - ₹1,600 (to Ahmedabad Junction)
  • Vadodara: ~1,200 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹3,500 - ₹6,000 (to Vadodara Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,200 - ₹2,200 (24-27 hours)
    • Train: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (to Vadodara Junction)
  • Somnath: ~1,500 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹6,500 (to Diu Airport, then 85 km by road)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (28-33 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹1,800 (to Veraval Junction, then 7 km by road)
  • Kutch (Bhuj): ~1,600 km from LPU
    • Flight: ₹4,000 - ₹7,000 (to Bhuj Airport)
    • Bus: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 (30-35 hours)
    • Train: ₹1,000 - ₹1,800 (to Bhuj Railway Station)

Top Destinations in Gujarat

Ahmedabad

A UNESCO World Heritage City, known for its stunning architecture, vibrant festivals, and the Sabarmati Ashram, home to Mahatma Gandhi.

Vadodara

Known for the Laxmi Vilas Palace and rich Maratha history, Vadodara offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Somnath

Home to the famous Somnath Temple, a significant pilgrimage site, and a place of historical importance.

Kutch

Famous for the Rann of Kutch, a stunning white salt desert that hosts the annual Rann Utsav festival.
