Welcome to Tripura

Tripura, a beautiful state in the northeastern part of India, is known for its rich culture, diverse history, and scenic beauty. It offers a blend of natural wonders, historic sites, and vibrant traditions, making it an intriguing destination for travelers. The state is home to ancient temples, royal palaces, and lush green landscapes.

Top Destinations in Tripura

Agartala

Agartala is the capital city of Tripura, known for its historical landmarks like Ujjayanta Palace and temples such as the Jagannath Temple. The city offers a rich cultural experience and serves as a gateway to exploring the state's heritage.

Ujjayanta Palace

The Ujjayanta Palace is an iconic historical building located in Agartala. This grand structure, built in the 19th century, showcases a blend of Mughal and European architecture and is a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

Neermahal

Neermahal, or the "Water Palace," is located on the Rudrasagar Lake and is a unique blend of Hindu and Mughal architectural styles. It was the summer palace of the Tripuri kings and is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Tripura.

Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary

Situated near Agartala, the Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary is home to various species of flora and fauna, including endangered species like the clouded leopard. The sanctuary offers a serene environment for nature lovers and bird watchers.
